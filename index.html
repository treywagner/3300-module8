About Trey

I grew up in Louisa County, an amalgamation of a few small farming communities (Letts, Grandview, Fruitland, Wapello, Columbus Junction). I graduated from Louisa-Muscatine High School, then from Muscatine Community College with an Associate Degree. I transfered to the Univerity of Iowa in 2023, and am on track to graduate in December of 2025 with a Bachelor's in Accounting and Business Analytics and Information Systems. I enjoy weightlifting and a little bit of running from time to time. I love gaming with my friends when I can. I enjoy RPG's and sandbox games like Skyrim and Minecraft (very basic, I know).
